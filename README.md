🌍 **From Raw Data to Real Insights – My Air Quality Data Analysis Project**

During my internship with the All India Council for Technical Education under the Microsoft Elevate Internship Program, I had the opportunity to work on a project that focuses on a real-world problem we experience every day — **air pollution**.

📊 **Project:** Air Quality Data Analysis & Visualization Dashboard

Air quality data contains thousands of records of environmental parameters, but without proper analysis it is difficult to identify patterns, trends, and key pollution factors. My goal was to transform this raw data into meaningful insights through an **interactive analytics dashboard**.

💡 **Problem**
Large environmental datasets exist, but decision-makers and the public often struggle to quickly understand:
• When pollution levels peak
• Which pollutants contribute the most
• How environmental factors impact air quality

⚙ **Solution**
I developed a **data analytics dashboard** that analyzes historical air quality data and visualizes pollution patterns in a clear and interactive way.

🛠 **Tools Used**
• Microsoft Excel – Dataset management
• Power Query – Data cleaning & transformation
• Microsoft Power BI – Dashboard development & visualization

📈 **Key Insights from the Data**
• Average **Air Quality Index (AQI): 249.60** — indicating poor air quality conditions.
• **PM10 and PM2.5** are the major contributors to pollution levels.
• Pollution patterns vary across **days and months**, showing seasonal impact.
• Environmental parameters such as **temperature, humidity, and pressure** influence pollution levels.

📊 **Dashboard Highlights**
✔ Daily air quality trend analysis
✔ Pollutant distribution visualization
✔ Monthly temperature pattern analysis
✔ Gas emission trends across time
✔ Interactive filters for deeper data exploration
